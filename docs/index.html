About me

I recently obtained the Ph.D. degree from Université Laval, under supervision of Prof. Christian Gagné and Prof. Boyu Wang.

My current research interests lie in both theoretical and practical aspects of machine learning. Particularly, I am interested in the distribution shift and its application in learning limited label data (transfer learning, meta-learning and active learning), algorithmic fairness and health.
