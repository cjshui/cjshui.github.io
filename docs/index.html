About me

I am currently a Postdoc in McGill University, under the supervision of Prof. Tal Arbel. I recently obtained Ph.D. degree from Université Laval, under the supervision of Prof. Christian Gagné and Prof. Boyu Wang.

My current research interests lie in both theoretical and practical aspects of machine learning. In particular, I am interested in distribution shift and its applications in learning limited label data (e.g., transfer learning, meta-learning and active learning), algorithmic fairness and health.
